Editorial & Considered

Tone. Stripe Press, the FT weekend, Linear at its most restrained. The Control Plane treats the developer as a reader. Generous whitespace; one serif italic that does emotional work; one ochre accent; one rust for warnings.

Type. Fraunces (variable serif, italic for display + supertitle) · Geist (body) · JetBrains Mono (IDs, code, telemetry only).
Density. Airy 8px grid; hairline rules instead of borders; numbers oversized and treated as headlines.
Motion. 200ms ease-out fade-and-slide. Pulse on Running. Never bounce, never elastic.
