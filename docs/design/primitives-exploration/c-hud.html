HUD // Mission Control

Tone. A cockpit panel. The Control Plane is your console; each Project is a craft you're monitoring. Frosted glass panels float over a gradient mesh; cyan is the primary signal, coral is danger, mint is verified, amber is in-flight. Inspired by Vercel, Linear's command palette, NASA EDL ops screens — without the purple-gradient cliché.

Type. IBM Plex Sans Condensed (display, ALL CAPS tracked) · Inter Tight (body) · JetBrains Mono (telemetry and IDs).
Density. Medium. Notched corners on every interactive surface (clip-path); 1px cyan strokes with a glow on focus.
Motion. 120ms hover cyan-flood. 1.2s pulse on Running. Skeleton sweeps a scanline left to right at 1.8s.
